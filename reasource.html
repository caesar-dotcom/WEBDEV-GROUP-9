  • Job offer & requirements

    • this is to tell the employees the type of job the employer is offering such as web developer job, car washer job ,sectary job etc and also to show the necessary requirements such as employees expirence with the precises job, employees gender, locatin etc in other to check the qualification of the job seekers

    • The employees needs to know the jobs location before they apply for the job
